
BANKING POLICY MANUAL

ACCOUNT POLICIES

1. Minimum balance requirement: Customers must maintain a minimum balance of $500 in their accounts at all times. Failure to maintain the minimum balance may result in monthly maintenance charges.

2. Account types available: The bank offers Checking Accounts, Savings Accounts, Business Accounts, Money Market Accounts, and Student Accounts. Each account type has different features, fees, and benefits.

3. Monthly maintenance fee: A maintenance fee of $10 per month applies to standard checking accounts. The fee is waived if the average monthly balance exceeds $5,000.

4. Account opening requirements: Customers must provide a valid government-issued photo ID, proof of address, tax identification number, and an initial deposit of at least $100.

5. Account closure policy: Customers wishing to close an account must provide a written request. The account should remain active for at least 30 days after opening. Remaining funds will be transferred or issued via cashier's check.

6. Joint accounts: Joint account holders share equal responsibility and access rights. Any account holder may initiate transactions unless otherwise specified.

7. Dormant accounts: Accounts with no activity for 12 consecutive months may be classified as dormant. Additional verification may be required to reactivate such accounts.

8. Statement generation: Monthly statements are generated electronically by default. Customers may request paper statements for an additional fee.

9. Beneficiary registration: Customers are encouraged to nominate beneficiaries to simplify inheritance procedures.

10. Account upgrades: Customers may request upgrades between account types subject to eligibility requirements.

TRANSFER POLICIES

1. Domestic transfer limit: Customers may transfer up to $10,000 per transaction.

2. Daily transfer limit: The maximum transfer amount allowed in a single day is $25,000.

3. Weekly transfer limit: Customers may not exceed $100,000 in transfers within a seven-day period.

4. Processing times: Transfers submitted before 3 PM are processed on the same business day. Requests submitted after 3 PM are processed on the next business day.

5. Internal transfers: Transfers between accounts within the same bank are processed instantly and are free of charge.

6. External transfers: Transfers to other banks incur a service fee of $2.50 per transaction.

7. International transfers: International transfers are available to approved countries. A service fee of $15 applies per transaction.

8. Verification requirements: Transfers above $1,000 require email or SMS confirmation.

9. Transfer cancellations: Transfers may be canceled before processing begins. Processed transactions cannot be canceled.

10. Recipient verification: Customers are responsible for ensuring recipient account information is accurate.

11. High-risk transfers: Additional verification procedures may apply for unusually large transactions.

12. Scheduled transfers: Customers may schedule recurring transfers for future dates.

13. Transfer disputes: Disputed transfers are reviewed within 5 business days.

14. Holiday processing: Transfers submitted during public holidays are processed on the next working day.

15. Compliance review: Certain international transfers may be delayed for regulatory review.

REFUND POLICIES

1. Refund eligibility: Refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of the original transaction date.

2. Refund processing: Approved refunds are processed within 3–5 business days.

3. Disputed transactions: Disputes are investigated within 10 business days.

4. Refund amount: Eligible refunds include the original transaction amount and applicable accrued interest.

5. Refund method: Refunds are credited to the original payment method whenever possible.

6. Partial refunds: Partial refunds may be approved when only part of a transaction is disputed.

7. Merchant disputes: Merchant-related disputes require supporting documentation.

8. Duplicate transactions: Duplicate charges are automatically reviewed and corrected when identified.

9. Chargeback requests: Customers may request chargebacks for unauthorized transactions.

10. Refund notifications: Customers receive email notifications once refunds are processed.

11. Refund tracking: Refund status may be tracked through online banking.

12. International transaction refunds: Additional processing time may apply for international transactions.

13. Service refunds: Fees charged in error will be refunded after investigation.

14. Escalation process: Unresolved disputes may be escalated to the dispute resolution team.

15. Documentation requirements: Supporting evidence may be requested for dispute investigations.

FRAUD PROTECTION POLICIES

1. Transaction monitoring: All transactions are monitored continuously for suspicious activity.

2. Fraud alerts: Customers receive automatic notifications for unusual transactions.

3. Verification threshold: Transactions exceeding $1,000 require additional verification.

4. Fraud response time: Reported fraud incidents receive an initial response within 24 hours.

5. Liability protection: Customer liability is limited to $50 when unauthorized activity is reported promptly.

6. Account freezes: Accounts suspected of compromise may be temporarily frozen.

7. Investigation process: Fraud investigations are conducted thoroughly using transaction records and security logs.

8. Identity verification: Additional identification may be required during investigations.

9. Suspicious login detection: Logins from unknown locations may trigger security alerts.

10. Device monitoring: Device fingerprinting technology helps identify suspicious activity.

11. Card protection: Lost or stolen cards should be reported immediately.

12. Risk scoring: Transactions are assigned fraud risk scores automatically.

13. Law enforcement cooperation: The bank cooperates with law enforcement when required.

14. Customer education: Security awareness information is provided regularly.

15. Fraud recovery: Recovery efforts are initiated whenever possible.

ACCOUNT SECURITY POLICIES

1. Two-factor authentication is available for all customers.

2. Passwords must contain at least 8 characters, one uppercase letter, one number, and one special character.

3. Passwords should not be shared with anyone.

4. Session timeout occurs after 15 minutes of inactivity.

5. Login alerts are sent via email and SMS.

6. Trusted device registration is available.

7. Customers should update passwords every 90 days.

8. Security questions may be required for identity verification.

9. Login attempts are limited to prevent brute-force attacks.

10. Encrypted communication is used for all online banking activities.

11. Customers should avoid accessing banking services on public computers.

12. Browser compatibility requirements are published regularly.

13. Security updates are deployed routinely.

14. Security incidents are reviewed by dedicated teams.

15. Unauthorized access attempts are logged and monitored.

2. Loan tenure ranges from 12 to 84 months.

3. Interest rates depend on credit history and risk assessment.

4. Processing fees may apply.

5. Early repayment is permitted after six months.

6. Loan applications require proof of income.

7. Credit checks are performed for all applicants.

8. Employment verification may be required.

9. Co-applicants may improve approval chances.

10. Late payment penalties apply after the grace period.

11. Loan statements are available online.

12. Customers may request loan restructuring under qualifying circumstances.

13. Auto-debit payment options are available.

14. Loan closure certificates are issued within 7 business days.

15. Delinquent loans may be referred for collection procedures.
